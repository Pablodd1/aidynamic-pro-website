Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers — no sales speak.

How much does AI automation cost for a small business? +
Our quick-win automations start at $2,000. A full AI Operating System typically ranges from $5,000-$15,000. Most clients see ROI within 30-60 days through reduced labor costs and increased efficiency. We always start with an audit so we can give you a precise quote based on your specific needs.
How long does it take to implement AI automation? +
Simple chatbots and basic workflows go live in 1-2 weeks. A comprehensive AI Operating System with multiple workflows takes 4-8 weeks from discovery to launch. We work with your team to ensure minimal disruption to current operations.
Can AI work with my existing systems? +
Yes. We specialize in integrating AI with popular CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), communication platforms (Slack, Teams), and business tools you already use. No need to replace your tech stack — we enhance it.
Do I need a tech team to maintain AI systems? +
No. We build systems that are easy to maintain and provide ongoing support. Most clients manage their AI systems with minimal technical knowledge, and we're always available for updates and optimizations through our monthly check-ins.
What's included in the AI Opportunity Audit? +
The audit includes a 1-3 day analysis of your operations (on-site for Miami-area businesses), interviews with key team members, workflow mapping, and a detailed report with prioritized automation opportunities ranked by impact and effort. You'll receive estimated time savings, recommended tools, and an implementation roadmap.

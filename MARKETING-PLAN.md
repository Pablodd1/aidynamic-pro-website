## Phase 2: Outreach Launch (Week 2)

### Email Campaign
- **Tool:** Instantly.ai ($37/month)
- **Volume:** Start 20/day → Scale to 50/day
- **Days:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM EST
- **Target:** 40 companies from `outreach-contacts.csv`

### LinkedIn Outreach
- **Tool:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($99/month) or manual
- **Volume:** 20 connection requests/day
- **Message:** Personalized with company detail
- **Follow-up:** Day 2, Day 7

### Content Marketing
- **LinkedIn:** 3 posts/week (Mon/Wed/Fri)
- **Blog:** Share ROI calculator in groups
- **Video:** Post Loom demos on LinkedIn

---

## Phase 3: Optimize & Scale (Weeks 3-4)

### Analyze Metrics
- Email open rates (target: >45%)
- Reply rates (target: >8%)
- Discovery calls booked (target: 5-10/week)

### A/B Testing
- Test 2 subject lines per template
- Test send times (AM vs PM)
- Test CTA (Calendly vs email reply)

### Scale What Works
- Increase email volume on winning templates
- Add more targets in winning industries
- Create industry-specific landing pages
